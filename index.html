About

I’m Julie Morris, a Front End Developer based in Ventura, CA.

I may not have grown up a “computer whiz,” but a drive for problem solving and constructing clever solutions has always played a pivotal role in my professional life. Whilst on maternity leave, I began to learn how to code to enhance my creative endeavors … and became utterly enamored with tech in the process! As a result, I completed a front end developer program at Skillcrush coding school, as well as extracurricular courses from Scrimba, and am now actively building beautiful, interactive web experiences full time.I can’t wait to work with you!
Fun fact! Prior to becoming a developer I enjoyed a rich and storied career as a wellness chef, cooking host, and New York Times bestselling author. I credit these extraordinary experiences with making me the amiable, detail-oriented, and green-smoothie-fueled coder I am today.
